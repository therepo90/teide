Pico del Teide free slots notifications

So you have planned your trip to Tenerife, but there are no free places to climb to the top of Pico Del Teide?

This app instantly notifies you by email when a seat becomes available for reservation and booking.

Just fill out the form, and the notification will come to your email. In the form, you specify the date range you are interested in.

It's free

Details:
- After receiving an email, you have to go to the site yourself and book the date.
- Subscription lasts for a maximum of 14 days or 10 email opportunities.
- Observing the operation of the application, I can say that several times a day a free date appears. Time to book it is about 2 to 60 minutes.
- There are many tickets for one appointment.
- The application checks only the current month and the next month.
- The app was originally created just for myself - after creation, I've booked the seat just the very same day. I decided to share it with others; maybe you'll find it handy.
